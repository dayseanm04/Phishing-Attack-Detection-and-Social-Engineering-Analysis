# Example 10 – Fake Norton Renewal

## Classification  
Suspicious Email (Phishing / Brand Impersonation)

## Scenario Summary  

This email claims to be from Norton, informing the recipient that their Norton LifeLock security subscription has been automatically renewed and that a charge will appear on their account. The recipient is encouraged to call a helpline number if they wish to cancel.

**Displayed Sender Name:** Norton Official  
**Sender Email:** notronofficial20@gmail.com
